What is Fragforce Reads?

Fragforce Reads is a volunteer initiative where Salesforce and Fragforce community members record themselves reading children's books aloud. Each physical book is donated to a Children's Miracle Network hospital with a QR code inside. When a child or caregiver scans the code, the story plays right in their browser.

How It Works

  1. A volunteer records a reading of a children's book
  2. The physical book is donated to a partner hospital
  3. A QR code inside the book links to the audio recording
  4. Kids scan and listen - no app, no login, just play

Get Involved

Want to record a book, help build the platform, or contribute in another way? Join us in #vto-book-reading-project on Slack.
